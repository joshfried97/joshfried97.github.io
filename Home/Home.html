Hello and Welcome to Villa 59!

This is a lovely little cafe situated in the village of Dechmont, West Lothian. It is run by your welcoming owner, Ann Downie.

Villa 59 offers a wide selection of hot and cold drinks, snacks, delicious filled rolls, full breakfasts and lunches. If you wish to see the menu you will find it on the "Menus" page above, if you want to find out more about me then visit the "Meet the owner page", to find where we are located and/or to contact me then please take a look at the "Contact us" page. Our "Social networking" page can be used for feedback on the design of the website.
